The data provided includes 10-15 parameters per day per year (arrival/departure time, vacations, sick days, time off, etc.) on 1000+ employees of the company. 
Filtered the most disciplined and undisciplined employees and divisions
Visualization is created with the analysis of weekdays and months when the most employess were late/absent (either for vacation or sick leave)
Identified the departments that tend to forgive employees for lack of discipline

This project used Microsoft PowerBI for data cleaning and transformation
